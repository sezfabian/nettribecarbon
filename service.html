Our Services

At Net Tribe Carbon, we are revolutionizing the carbon credit from the Electric Motorcycles industry.

Carbon Credit Wallet

At Net Tribe Carbon, we understand the value of your carbon credits. Our Carbon Credit Wallet ensures a secure and user-friendly experience for managing your carbon assets. Say goodbye to distractions and hello to seamless credit management.

E-Mobility IoT

Witness the synergy of IoT and Blockchain in our Electric Motorcycles. Our integration services provide real-time data on carbon emissions reduction, enhancing the environmental impact of our e-mobility initiatives.

Carbon Credit ICO

We provide you with the exciting opportunity to purchase and trade Carbon Credits. Our ICO (Initial Carbon Offering) is your gateway to invest in a more sustainable world while also participating in the carbon offsetting market.

Carbon Credit(DEX)

Experience the future of carbon credit trading on our decentralized platform. Trade verified carbon credits transparently and securely through our DEX. Transparently and securely trade verified carbon credits.

Asset-Backed Token

Carbon Credit Offsetting

Offset your carbon footprint with our comprehensive Carbon Credit Offset services. Calculate, purchase, and retire carbon credits seamlessly with real time data on our platform. Say no to hypothetical data.
